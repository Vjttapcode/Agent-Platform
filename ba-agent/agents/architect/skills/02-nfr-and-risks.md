# Skill: NFR Strategy & Technical Risk

**When:** addressing non-functional requirements and technical risk in the
architecture (part of Solutioning).

**How:**
- For each `NFR-###` (performance, security, availability, scalability,
  compliance…), state the concrete mechanism that satisfies it.
- Identify the technically hard or uncertain parts; rate likelihood × impact.
- For each High risk (`R-#`): the core challenge, what to spike/POC first, and a
  simpler fallback if the ideal approach proves infeasible.
- Be engineering-grounded and specific — avoid vague reassurances.
