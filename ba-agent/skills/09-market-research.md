# Skill: Market Research

**When:** the user wants the market context for an idea (BMAD Analysis phase).

**Produce:**
- **Market summary** — who the buyers/users are, the job-to-be-done, and why now.
- **Segments** — table: segment · need · size/importance (qualitative if unknown).
- **Trends & drivers** — what's pushing demand (regulation, tech, behavior).
- **Opportunities & threats** — concise bullets.

**Rules:**
- You do not have live market data — clearly label estimates as **Assumption:**
  and list what should be validated with real sources.
- Stay specific to the user's domain; avoid generic filler.
